NEWS

Phil Mickelson becomes oldest major winner: In May 2021, Phil Mickelson made history by becoming the oldest golfer to win a major championship. He won the PGA Championship at the age of 50, beating some of the top golfers in the world.

Hideki Matsuyama wins the Masters: In April 2021, Hideki Matsuyama became the first Japanese golfer to win the Masters. He finished the tournament with a score of 10 under par, holding off a late challenge from American golfer Will Zalatoris.

Tiger Woods' car accident: In February 2021, Tiger Woods was involved in a serious car accident that left him with multiple leg injuries. The golf world rallied around Woods, who is widely regarded as one of the greatest golfers of all time.

Dustin Johnson wins the FedEx Cup: In September 2020, Dustin Johnson won the FedEx Cup, which is awarded to the golfer with the most points over the course of the season. Johnson also won the Tour Championship, the final event of the season.

Women's golf continues to grow: The women's golf scene has been gaining more attention and recognition in recent years. In 2022, the Solheim Cup will be held in Spain, with the best women golfers from Europe and the United States competing against each other.
